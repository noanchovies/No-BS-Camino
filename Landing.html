Top 10 Myths Debunked
GEAR
COMMON MYTH

"Trail Runners vs. Boots"

The Reality

90% of veteran pilgrims now recommend non-waterproof trail runners. The Gore-tex membrane traps heat and sweat = sweat and blisters. While they excel in wet, muddy, or cold weather, they sacrifice breathability.

Source: r/CaminoDeSantiago Read Full Breakdown →
RULES
2025 UPDATE

"2025> Rule Changes (Compostela)"

The Reality

"One stamp a day is enough". Not anymore. You must get 2 stamps per day (one at lunch, one at sleep) or the Pilgrim Office will deny your Compostela.

Source: Pilgrim Office Read Full Breakdown →
LOGISTICS

"Book IN ADVANCEEEE via Booking.com."

The Reality

Nonsense. If you must..do it the day before.
Nowadays the best albergues aren't online and use WhatsApp to save on fees. Install "Gronze" (the bible). If you see a line of backpacks at 1 PM, that's the bed race.
The community has shifted from "Wise Pilgrim" to Gronze.com (best data) and Camino Ninja (most user-friendly).

Source: Veteran Walkers Read Full Breakdown →
MONEY

"Costs & Logistics (Money vs Card)"

The Reality

The €50 Rule: The "Budget" Camino (€25/day) is almost dead due to inflation in Spain. The 2025/2026 "Standard" budget is now €50/day (Albergue + Menu del Dia + Coffee/Laundry).
Transport: Casa Ivar in Santiago is still the gold standard for shipping your suitcase ahead so it's waiting for you at the end.

Source: Experience Read Full Breakdown →
WEIGHT
COMMON MYTH

"Pack for every emergency."

The Reality

Your pack should be 10% of body weight (5-7kg).
If it's more, you're carrying fear. Light is right, there are plenty of shops every few KM to buy the essentials.

Source: Ivar Read Full Breakdown →
SLEEP
FEAR FACTOR

"Albergues are dirty."

The Reality

Even hotels get bed bugs. It's a volume issue. Permethrin is your best friend. Spray your bag before you leave home.

Source: Science Read Full Breakdown →
SAFETY

"It's dangerous solo."

The Reality

Statistically safer than your hometown. You will be "alone" for exactly 4 hours until you meet your "Tramily". Again - it's Spain not Antarctica, regardless of being Summer or Winter.

Source: Crime Stats Read Full Breakdown →
FITNESS

"I need to run marathons."

The Reality

The first week is the training. Start slow. Rushing causes injuries, not fitness.
Reality Check: You'll be walking through mostly flat well maintained paths.
People need to chill (don't get me started on the "altitude drama")

Source: Physiology Read Full Breakdown →
TECH

"I'll use Google Maps."

The Reality

Google sends you to highways. Use Mapy.cz or Organic Maps for trails. Buy a cheap Spanish Sim in any corner shop. Europeans roam for free, look it up.

Source: Navigation Read Full Breakdown →
THE VIBE
TOXIC MYTH

"The last 100km isn't real."

The Reality

Ignore the gatekeepers. Whether you walk 800km or 100km, you're already doing more than the internet experts. Who cares anyway
