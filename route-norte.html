Camino Norte

The Northern Way

The "Hard Mode" route. Famous for rugged coastlines, Michelin-star food, and punishing daily elevation gains.

825 km
32-37 Days
Moderate Traffic
Irún → Santiago

The Route Sections

Basque Country
The Workout

Steep coastal ridges, amazing food, expensive beds.

Cantabria
The Asphalt

More road walking, stunning beaches, medieval towns.

Asturias
The Rural

Green pastures, cider houses, split to Primitivo.

Galicia
The Convergence

Turning south, inland forests, joining the Francés crowds.

Best For

  • • Strong walkers (cumulative elevation is brutal).
  • • Foodies (San Sebastián & Bilbao are world-class).
  • • Summer pilgrims (cooler coastal weather).

Skip If

  • • You have knee issues (constant up/down).
  • • You are on a strict budget (Basque country is pricey).
  • • You want the "classic" social vibe (it's quieter).
